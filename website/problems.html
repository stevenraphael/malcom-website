Problem 4

Alice, Bob, and Carlos each use a diﬀerent number base, and one of them always tells the truth, while the other two always lie. These are the statements they make:

Alice: I use Base 5. In my base, 3 + 3 = 11.

Bob: I use Base 10. In my base, 5 + 5 = 12.

Carlos: I use Base 12. In my base, 8 + 8 = 13.

If all three people use a positive integral base, what base does the truth-teller use, expressed in decimal?
